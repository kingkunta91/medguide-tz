MedGuide TZ Mobile App

Open the mobile app here: medguide-tz-mobile/index.html

  1. For phone installation, host the medguide-tz-mobile folder over HTTPS or localhost.
  2. Open it in Chrome/Edge/Safari on the phone.
  3. Choose Install app or Add to Home Screen.
  4. After first load, the app shell is cached for offline use.

Built by Dr. Michael Wambura, a medical doctor at Naturra Wellness Hub. Protocol summaries are sourced from Tanzania Ministry of Health guidance and verified WHO emergency, maternal, child, and mental health resources.
