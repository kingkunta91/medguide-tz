MedGuide TZ

Medical emergency protocols with in-protocol doses, plus calculators and scores for clinical practice in Tanzania. Updated source review: 1 June 2026.

Use as a clinical aid only. Medicine doses appear inside emergency protocols, not as a standalone formulary. Confirm contraindications, stock formulations, pregnancy status, allergies, renal function, and local facility policy before treatment. Escalate early for critically ill patients.
Built by Dr. Michael Wambura, a medical doctor at Naturra Wellness Hub. Main references include Tanzania Ministry of Health Standard Treatment Guidelines and National Essential Medicines List, WHO Emergency Care Toolkit, WHO/ICRC Basic Emergency Care, WHO Interagency Integrated Triage Tool, WHO Emergency Unit Checklist, WHO mhGAP Humanitarian Intervention Guide, WHO Managing Complications in Pregnancy and Childbirth, and WHO Pocket Book of Hospital Care for Children.
