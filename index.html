MedGuide TZ was created by Dr. Michael Wambura, a medical doctor at Naturra Wellness Hub, as a practical bedside aid for emergency protocols, calculators, and scores in Tanzanian clinical settings.

The summaries are intended to support, not replace, clinical judgement. They should be checked against current local hospital policy, Tanzania Ministry of Health guidance, and the linked WHO source documents before institutional deployment.

MedGuide TZ is a clinical reference aid for emergencies, calculators, and scores, built by Dr. Michael Wambura, a medical doctor at Naturra Wellness Hub. Medicine guidance is limited to doses embedded inside emergency protocols; confirm local protocols, patient factors, formulations, and current Tanzania Ministry of Health and WHO guidance before clinical action.
